About Us

Welcome to HaveFriends! We are so happy to see you here. HaveFriends is your place of safety for animals in need of a home. The vision behind this platform has always been simple but powerful; it aims to design a bridge between pets without homes and persons and families willing to open their hearts and homes to animals in need.

As we believe, all animals deserve a second chance to live. We aim to have that number reduced by allowing the easy way of adoption, full of kindness. Whether you're searching for a playful puppy or a gentle senior cat, or anything in between, we have a little something to help you take that next step.

What sets us apart is our commitment to the pet and its potential new owner. Every adoption is customize to ensure that the pet and new family are completely suitable. By partnering with local shelters, rescues, and foster homes, we grant every animal the care and attention they completely deserve as they await their forever home.

It is not just a platform for pet adoption but an online community of animal lovers who wants to bring the change. It will have everything from resources for education and opportunities for volunteering to events for the community to create awareness on responsible pet ownership and benefits of adoption.

Thank you for taking part in this journey, as we can do that together which ensures every pet has a home it deserves. So if you're ready to make your difference, start navigating our site to experience the magic of welcoming a new furry friend into your life.

Founded in 2024, Have Friends is dedicated to creating a safe and easy-to-use platform for pet adoption. Join us in making a difference.

One adoption at a time.
